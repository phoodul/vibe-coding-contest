## 핵심 명제

> **1~2 음절 = -er, 3+ 음절 = more. 둘이 비교 + than.**

비교급 형태와 than 짝꿍.

---

## 설명

### 비교급 만들기

| 음절 | 규칙 | 예 |
|---|---|---|
| 1음절 | + er | tall → taller |
| 1음절 -e | + r | nice → nicer |
| 1음절 단모음+자음 | 자음 중복 + er | big → bigger |
| 2음절 -y | -y → -i + er | happy → happier |
| 2음절 기타 | + er 또는 more (둘 다) | clever → cleverer/more clever |
| 3음절 이상 | more + 원급 | beautiful → more beautiful |

### 불규칙 비교급

| 원급 | 비교급 |
|---|---|
| good / well | better |
| bad / ill | worse |
| many / much | more |
| little | less |
| far | farther (거리) / further (정도) |
| old | older / elder (나이·서열) |

### 형태 + than

```
✓ Tom is taller than Jerry.
✓ She is more beautiful than me.
✓ This book is better than that one.
✓ My grandfather is older than my father.
```

### less + 원급 + than — 더 적게 ~한

`-er` 의 반대 의미.

```
✓ He is less tall than me. (= 그는 나보다 덜 크다 = 나보다 작다)
✓ This is less interesting than that.
```

less + 원급 vs 비교급 + than 차이는 거의 없으나 less 가 약간 격식·완곡.

### 비교급 강조 — much / even / far / a lot

`much, even, far, a lot, still` + 비교급. **very 는 X**.

```
✓ He is much taller than me.
✗ He is very taller than me. (very + 비교급 X)

✓ This is even better.
✓ The new model is far more expensive.
```

`very` 는 원급에만, `much` 는 비교급에.

### 비교 대상 — 같은 격·구조 일치

```
✗ My hair is longer than my sister. (내 머리 vs 내 동생 사람 — 비대칭)
✓ My hair is longer than my sister's. (내 머리 vs 동생의 머리)
✓ My hair is longer than that of my sister. (격식)
```

비교 대상이 같아야 자연.

### 점점 더 ~ — 비교급 and 비교급

```
✓ It is getting colder and colder. (점점 더 추워진다)
✓ She became more and more beautiful.
```

### the + 비교급, the + 비교급 — ~할수록 더 ~

```
✓ The more you study, the smarter you become.
(공부할수록 더 똑똑해진다)

✓ The harder you work, the more you earn.
```

`the + 비교급` 두 절을 묶음. 시험 단골.

### no + 비교급 + than — 라틴 비교

`no + 비교급 + than` = ~만큼 (강한 부정).

```
✓ He is no taller than me. (= 나만큼 작다)
✓ A whale is no more a fish than a horse is.
(고래가 어류가 아닌 것은 말이 어류가 아닌 것과 같다)
```

좀 어려운 패턴 — 고급 시험.

---

## 대표 문장 (외우기)

> **The more you study, the better your grades become — much better than before.**
>
> 더 많이 공부할수록 성적은 더 좋아진다 — 전보다 훨씬 더 좋아진다.

`The more ~, the better ~` (~할수록 더 ~) + `much better` (much 강조). 두 핵심 패턴.

## 실전 문제 5개

### 문제 1.
다음 빈칸에 알맞은 것은?

`Tom is _____ Jerry.`

① taller  ② more tall  ③ taller than  ④ more taller than

### 문제 2.
어법상 옳은 것은?

① He is very taller than me.
② He is much taller than me.
③ He is more taller than me.
④ He is more tall than me.

### 문제 3.
다음 중 어법상 **틀린** 문장은?

① It is getting colder and colder.
② This book is more interesting than that one.
③ My hair is longer than my sister.
④ She is older than her brother.

### 문제 4.
빈칸에 들어갈 적절한 것은?

`The harder you study, _____ you become.`

① smarter  ② the smarter  ③ more smart  ④ the more smart

### 문제 5.
다음 우리말을 영어로 옮길 때 가장 적절한 것은?

> 이 책은 그 책보다 훨씬 더 어렵다.

① This book is very more difficult than that book.
② This book is much difficult than that book.
③ This book is much more difficult than that book.
④ This book is more difficult than much that book.

---

## 정답 + 해설

### 1. ③ taller than

비교급 + than.

### 2. ② He is much taller than me.

very 는 원급, much 가 비교급 강조.

### 3. ③ My hair is longer than my sister.

비교 대상 비대칭. → `my sister's hair` 또는 `my sister's`.

### 4. ② the smarter

`The 비교급 ~, the 비교급 ~` 패턴.

### 5. ③ This book is much more difficult than that book.

3음절 → more difficult. 강조 → much.
