## 핵심 명제

> **주절이 과거면 종속절도 시제를 한 단계 뒤로 (backshift). 보편적 진리는 예외.**

직접화법 → 간접화법 변환의 핵심 룰. 화법은 단원 12 에서.

---

## 설명

영어는 주절과 종속절의 시제를 일치시킨다. 주절이 과거면 종속절도 한 단계 뒤로.

### Backshift 룰

| 주절 현재 → 과거 시 종속절 | 변화 |
|---|---|
| 현재 (V) | → 과거 (V-ed) |
| 현재진행 (is V-ing) | → 과거진행 (was V-ing) |
| 현재완료 (have p.p.) | → 과거완료 (had p.p.) |
| 단순과거 (V-ed) | → 과거완료 (had p.p.) |
| will | → would |
| can | → could |
| may | → might |
| must | → had to |

### 예시 비교

**주절 현재**:

```
He says he is hungry.
He says he ate lunch.
He says he will come.
```

**주절 과거** (모두 한 단계 뒤로):

```
He said he was hungry. (is → was)
He said he had eaten lunch. (ate → had eaten)
He said he would come. (will → would)
```

### 예외 1 — 보편적 진리·과학적 사실

언제 들어도 변하지 않는 사실은 backshift 안 함.

```
✗ The teacher said that the earth went around the sun.
✓ The teacher said that the earth goes around the sun. (현재 유지)

✓ He said that water boils at 100°C.
```

### 예외 2 — 현재도 변함없는 일

현재까지도 사실인 일은 backshift 선택적.

```
✓ She said she likes coffee. (지금도 좋아함 — 현재 유지)
✓ She said she liked coffee. (backshift 도 OK)
```

### 예외 3 — 역사적 사실

확정된 역사 사건은 단순과거 그대로 (과거완료로 안 가도 OK).

```
✓ He told us that World War II ended in 1945.
✓ He told us that World War II had ended in 1945. (과거완료도 OK 지만 단순과거 자연)
```

### 시간·장소 표시 변화

직접화법 → 간접화법 시 시간·장소 표현도 변환.

| 직접 | 간접 |
|---|---|
| now | then |
| today | that day |
| tomorrow | the next day |
| yesterday | the day before |
| ago | before |
| this | that |
| here | there |

```
He said, "I will come tomorrow."
→ He said (that) he would come the next day.
```

### 의문문에 backshift 도 동일

```
She asked, "Do you have time?"
→ She asked if I had time.

She asked, "When will you arrive?"
→ She asked when I would arrive.
```

(자세히는 단원 12 화법에서)

## 대표 문장 (외우기)

> **He said that the earth goes around the sun, and that he had finished his work.**
>
> 그는 지구가 태양 주위를 돈다고 말했고, 또 자기 일을 다 끝냈다고 말했다.

`said` (주절 과거) + `goes` (보편 진리는 현재 유지) + `had finished` (단순과거 → 과거완료 backshift). 룰 + 예외가 한 문장에.

## 실전 문제 5개

### 문제 1.
다음 빈칸에 알맞은 것은?

`She said that she _____ tired.`

① is  ② was  ③ has been  ④ will be

### 문제 2.
어법상 옳은 것은?

① The teacher said that water boiled at 100°C.
② The teacher said that water boils at 100°C.
③ The teacher said that water has boiled at 100°C.
④ The teacher says that water boiled at 100°C.

### 문제 3.
다음 중 시제 일치가 어색한 것은?

① He said that he would call me later.
② She said that she could speak French.
③ I thought that he is right.
④ They told me that they had already eaten.

### 문제 4.
다음 직접화법을 간접화법으로 바꿀 때 가장 자연스러운 것은?

`He said, "I will leave tomorrow."`

① He said that he will leave tomorrow.
② He said that he would leave tomorrow.
③ He said that he would leave the next day.
④ He said that he leaves the next day.

### 문제 5.
다음 우리말을 영어로 옮길 때 가장 적절한 것은?

> 그녀는 자기가 어제 영화를 봤다고 말했다.

① She said that she watches a movie yesterday.
② She said that she watched a movie yesterday.
③ She said that she had watched a movie the day before.
④ She said that she will watch a movie yesterday.

## 정답 + 해설

### 1. ② was

said (과거) + 현재 → 과거 backshift → was. ① backshift 안 됨. ③ 완료 부적절. ④ 미래 X.

### 2. ② The teacher said that water boils at 100°C.

보편 진리는 backshift 예외 → 현재 유지. ① backshift 도 가능하지만 정형 답은 현재. ③ 현재완료 X. ④ 주절 현재인데 종속 과거 어색.

### 3. ③ I thought that he is right.

thought (과거) + is → was 로 backshift 필요. → `I thought that he was right`. (또는 지금도 옳다는 어감 강조 시 is 도 가능).

### 4. ③ He said that he would leave the next day.

will → would, tomorrow → the next day. ① will 그대로 X. ② tomorrow 그대로. ④ leaves + the next day 시제 X.

### 5. ③ She said that she had watched a movie the day before.

said (과거) + watched (과거) → 과거완료 (한 단계 뒤). yesterday → the day before. ② 단순과거 그대로 + yesterday — 일상에선 OK 지만 정형 backshift 답은 ③.
