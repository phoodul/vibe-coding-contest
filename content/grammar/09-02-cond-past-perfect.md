## 핵심 명제

> **과거 사실의 반대 = If 주어 + had p.p., 주어 + would have + p.p.**

가정법 과거 (현재 반대) 의 시제를 한 단계 더 뒤로.

---

## 설명

### 형태

`If 주어 + had + p.p., 주어 + would/could/might + have + p.p.`

```
✓ If I had studied harder, I would have passed the exam.
(공부 안 했다 → 만약 했다면, 합격했을 텐데)

✓ If she had known the truth, she would have been sad.
(몰랐다 → 만약 알았다면, 슬펐을 텐데)
```

### 가정법 과거 vs 가정법 과거완료 비교

```
가정법 과거 (현재 반대):
If I were rich, I would buy a house. (지금 부자 아님)

가정법 과거완료 (과거 반대):
If I had been rich, I would have bought a house. (그때 부자 아니었음)
```

시제가 한 단계씩 뒤로.

### 직설법 변환

```
가정법 과거완료: If I had studied harder, I would have passed.
직설법: As I didn't study harder, I didn't pass.
(또는: I didn't study harder, so I didn't pass.)
```

과거의 부정.

### 주절의 조동사 — would/could/might + have + p.p.

| 형태 | 의미 |
|---|---|
| would have done | ~했을 텐데 |
| could have done | ~할 수 있었을 텐데 |
| might have done | ~했을지도 모를 텐데 |

```
✓ If I had known, I would have told you. (말해줬을 텐데)
✓ If I had had time, I could have helped. (도와줄 수 있었을 텐데)
✓ If we had left earlier, we might have caught the bus. (잡았을지도)
```

(4-6 `modal-perfect` 의 should/could/must have + p.p. 와 같은 구조)

### 자주 시험에 나오는 패턴

```
✓ If you had called me, I would have come.
(전화 안 했다 → 만약 했다면, 왔을 텐데)

✓ If she hadn't been late, she wouldn't have missed the train.
(늦었다 → 만약 늦지 않았다면, 기차 놓치지 않았을 텐데)

✓ Had I known earlier, I would have decided differently.
(if 생략 도치형 — 9-6)
```

### 도치형 (if 생략 + Had + 주어 + p.p.)

```
✓ Had I studied harder, I would have passed.
= If I had studied harder, I would have passed.

✓ Had it not been for your help, I would have failed.
= If it had not been for your help, I would have failed.
```

(9-6 에서 자세히)

### 가정법 과거 vs 가정법 과거완료 — 시제 표 정리

| 종류 | if 절 | 주절 | 의미 |
|---|---|---|---|
| 가정법 과거 | If + 과거 동사 (were) | would + 원형 | 현재 반대 |
| 가정법 과거완료 | If + had p.p. | would have + p.p. | 과거 반대 |

---

## 대표 문장 (외우기)

> **If I had studied harder, I would have passed the exam.**
>
> 만약 내가 더 열심히 공부했다면, 시험에 합격했을 텐데.

`If 주어 + had p.p.` + `주어 + would have + p.p.` 가정법 과거완료의 가장 표준 패턴.

---

## 실전 문제 5개

### 문제 1.
다음 빈칸에 알맞은 것은?

`If I _____ the truth, I would have told you.`

① know  ② knew  ③ had known  ④ have known

### 문제 2.
어법상 옳은 것은?

① If she came earlier, she would have met him.
② If she had come earlier, she would meet him.
③ If she had come earlier, she would have met him.
④ If she had come earlier, she will meet him.

### 문제 3.
다음 가정법 문장의 직설법 변환으로 옳은 것은?

`If he had studied harder, he would have passed.`

① He didn't study hard, so he didn't pass.
② He doesn't study hard, so he doesn't pass.
③ He studies hard, so he passes.
④ He studied hard, so he passed.

### 문제 4.
다음 중 어법상 **틀린** 문장은?

① If I had had more time, I would have helped you.
② If you had told me, I would have come.
③ If she had been here, she would explain it.
④ If we hadn't left late, we would have caught the train.

### 문제 5.
다음 우리말을 영어로 옮길 때 가장 적절한 것은?

> 내가 너의 충고를 들었더라면, 그런 실수를 안 했을 텐데.

① If I listen to your advice, I will not make such a mistake.
② If I listened to your advice, I would not make such a mistake.
③ If I had listened to your advice, I would not have made such a mistake.
④ If I have listened to your advice, I would not have made such a mistake.

---

## 정답 + 해설

### 1. ③ had known

주절이 would have + p.p. → 가정법 과거완료 if 절은 had + p.p.

### 2. ③ If she had come earlier, she would have met him.

가정법 과거완료 = had p.p. + would have p.p.

### 3. ① He didn't study hard, so he didn't pass.

가정법 과거완료 = 과거 부정 사실.

### 4. ③ If she had been here, she would explain it.

가정법 과거완료 → 주절도 would have + p.p. → `would have explained`. 시제 일치 X.

### 5. ③ If I had listened to your advice, I would not have made such a mistake.

과거 사실 반대 → 가정법 과거완료.
