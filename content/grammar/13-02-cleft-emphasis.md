## 핵심 명제

> **It is + 강조 부분 + that + 나머지. 주어/목적어/부사구를 다 강조 가능.**

명사·부사구는 that, 사람은 who 도 가능.

---

## 설명

### It is ~ that 패턴

원래 문장에서 강조하고 싶은 부분을 `It is` 뒤로 빼고 나머지를 `that` 절에.

```
원문: Tom met Jane at the cafe yesterday.

강조 1 (주어 Tom): It was Tom that met Jane at the cafe yesterday.
강조 2 (목적어 Jane): It was Jane that Tom met at the cafe yesterday.
강조 3 (장소 the cafe): It was at the cafe that Tom met Jane yesterday.
강조 4 (시간 yesterday): It was yesterday that Tom met Jane at the cafe.
```

거의 모든 부분 강조 가능 — 동사 자체만 빼고.

### that 대신 who/which/when/where

| 강조 부분 | that 대안 |
|---|---|
| 사람 | who |
| 사물 | which (드물게) |
| 시간 | when |
| 장소 | where |

```
✓ It was Tom who met Jane.
✓ It was the cafe where Tom met Jane.
✓ It was yesterday when Tom met Jane.
```

`that` 만 써도 다 OK 라 구별 신경 안 써도 됨.

### 시제는 원문에 따라

| 원문 시제 | It is/was |
|---|---|
| 현재 | It is |
| 과거 | It was |

```
✓ It is John that loves her now. (현재)
✓ It was John that loved her then. (과거)
```

### 동사 강조는 do/does/did

동사를 강조할 때는 `It is ~ that` 못 씀. 대신 **do/does/did + 원형**.

```
원문: I love you.
강조: I do love you. (정말 사랑한다)

원문: She came yesterday.
강조: She did come yesterday.
```

3단·시제별 do 변형 (do/does/did).

### 의문문 강조 — Wh- + is it that ~?

```
원문: Where did you go?
강조: Where was it that you went?

원문: Why did he leave?
강조: Why was it that he left?
```

격식·강조.

### what + 절 강조 — 명사절

```
원문: What I want is your honesty.
강조 (이미 강조 형태): What I want is your honesty.

원문: She gave me this book.
강조: What she gave me was this book.
```

`What 절` 도 강조 효과 있음.

### 강조 vs 가주어 it — 형태 비슷, 의미 다름

| 형태 | 의미 |
|---|---|
| It is + 강조부분 + that | 강조구문 |
| It is + 형용사 + that | 가주어 (진주어 = that 절) |

```
강조: It is the book that I bought. (그 책을 샀다 — 강조)
가주어: It is true that he is honest. (그가 정직한 것 사실 — 가주어)
```

구별법: It is 와 that 사이를 빼고 의미 통하면 강조구문, 통하면 가주어.

```
"the book that I bought" → 의미 통함 → 강조
"true that he is honest" → It 빼면 어색 → 가주어
```

## 대표 문장 (외우기)

> **It was at the cafe that I met her yesterday — and I do love her.**
>
> 어제 카페에서 내가 그녀를 만난 것이다 — 그리고 정말 그녀를 사랑한다.

`It was at the cafe that` (장소 강조) + `do love` (동사 강조). 두 강조.

## 실전 문제 5개

### 문제 1.
다음 문장의 'Tom' 을 강조한 것은?

`Tom met Jane at the cafe yesterday.`

① It was Tom that met Jane at the cafe yesterday.
② It was Jane that Tom met at the cafe yesterday.
③ It was at the cafe that Tom met Jane yesterday.
④ It was yesterday that Tom met Jane at the cafe.

### 문제 2.
어법상 옳은 것은?

① I do loved you.
② I did loving you.
③ I do love you.
④ I am do love you.

### 문제 3.
다음 빈칸에 알맞은 것은?

`It was yesterday _____ I met her.`

① that  ② which  ③ what  ④ if

### 문제 4.
다음 두 문장의 의미가 같도록 빈칸에 알맞은 것은?

`I really like coffee.`
= `I _____ like coffee.`

① is  ② have  ③ do  ④ am

### 문제 5.
다음 우리말을 영어로 옮길 때 가장 적절한 것은?

> 내가 본 사람은 바로 그녀였다.

① It was she that I saw.
② It was her that I saw.
③ It was her who I saw.
④ ① ② ③ 모두 가능

---

## 정답 + 해설

### 1. ① It was Tom that met Jane at the cafe yesterday.

Tom 강조 → It was Tom that.

### 2. ③ I do love you.

동사 강조 = do/does/did + 원형. ① do + loved X. ② doing X. ④ am do X.

### 3. ① that

It is/was ~ that 강조구문. (when 도 가능하지만 that 가장 표준).

### 4. ③ do

really like = do like (강조).

### 5. ④ ① ② ③ 모두 가능

격식 = she 주격, 구어 = her 목적격. who 도 사람용 가능. 시험에선 모두 인정.
