## 핵심 명제

> **감정·관심 수동은 by 가 아닌 다른 전치사. 동사·형용사별 짝꿍을 외운다.**

표 한 장이 시험 답.

---

## 설명

수동태의 행위자는 보통 `by` 지만, 감정·상태·관심·재료 표현에는 다른 전치사가 고정.

### 8 대 짝꿍

| 표현 | 전치사 | 의미 |
|---|---|---|
| be interested in | in | ~에 관심 있다 |
| be surprised at | at | ~에 놀라다 |
| be pleased with | with | ~에 기뻐하다 |
| be satisfied with | with | ~에 만족하다 |
| be filled with | with | ~로 가득 차다 |
| be covered with | with | ~로 덮이다 |
| be known to | to | ~에게 알려지다 |
| be made of/from | of/from | ~로 만들어지다 |

### 예시

```
✓ I am interested in music.
✓ She was surprised at the news.
✓ He is pleased with the gift.
✓ We are satisfied with the result.
✓ The bottle is filled with water.
✓ The mountain is covered with snow.
✓ He is known to everyone.
```

### be made of / from / into — 재료 변형 정도

| 형태 | 의미 |
|---|---|
| be made of | 재료가 보임 (변형 작음) |
| be made from | 재료가 안 보임 (변형 큼) |
| be made into | 재료가 ~로 변형됨 |

```
✓ The desk is made of wood. (나무 보임)
✓ Wine is made from grapes. (포도 안 보임)
✓ Grapes are made into wine. (포도 → 와인 변형)
```

### be known — 4가지 의미

| 형태 | 의미 |
|---|---|
| be known to | ~에게 알려진 (대상) |
| be known for | ~로 유명한 (이유) |
| be known as | ~로서 알려진 (역할·이름) |
| be known by | ~로 판별되는 |

```
✓ He is known to everyone. (모두에게 알려짐)
✓ She is known for her kindness. (친절로 유명)
✓ He is known as a writer. (작가로서)
✓ A man is known by the company he keeps. (친구로 판별)
```

### 감정 형용사 — 사람 주어 + 전치사

(1-5 / 7-1 단원의 감정 형용사 -ed 형 — 사람이 받는 감정)

| 형태 | 전치사 |
|---|---|
| be excited about | about |
| be worried about | about |
| be afraid of | of |
| be tired of | of |
| be ashamed of | of |
| be proud of | of |
| be sick of | of |
| be amazed at/by | at/by |
| be disappointed with/in/at | with/in/at |
| be shocked at/by | at/by |

```
✓ I am excited about the trip.
✓ She is worried about her son.
✓ He is afraid of dogs.
✓ I am tired of waiting.
✓ She is proud of her achievements.
```

### 학생 단골 함정

```
✗ I am interested for music.
✓ I am interested in music.

✗ She is married with a doctor.
✓ She is married to a doctor. (married to)

✗ He is engaged with her.
✓ He is engaged to her. (engaged to)
```

`married to` / `engaged to` 도 시험 단골.

---

## 대표 문장 (외우기)

> **I am interested in music, surprised at his talent, and pleased with the concert.**
>
> 나는 음악에 관심 있고, 그의 재능에 놀랐고, 콘서트에 기뻤다.

`interested in` + `surprised at` + `pleased with`. 8 대 짝꿍 중 3개.

---

## 실전 문제 5개

### 문제 1.
다음 빈칸에 알맞은 것은?

`I am interested _____ Korean culture.`

① by  ② in  ③ at  ④ with

### 문제 2.
어법상 옳은 것은?

① The desk is made from wood.
② The desk is made of wood.
③ The desk is made into wood.
④ The desk is made by wood.

### 문제 3.
다음 중 어법상 **틀린** 문장은?

① She is married to a doctor.
② He is known for his kindness.
③ The mountain is covered with snow.
④ I am surprised by the news. → ②와 같은 의미: I am surprised at the news.

다음 중 어법상 틀린 것은?

① The bottle is filled by water.
② The room is filled with smoke.
③ He is known to many people.
④ Wine is made from grapes.

### 문제 4.
빈칸에 들어갈 적절한 것은?

`She is afraid _____ heights.`

① by  ② of  ③ in  ④ at

### 문제 5.
다음 우리말을 영어로 옮길 때 가장 적절한 것은?

> 그는 친절함으로 유명하다.

① He is known to his kindness.
② He is known for his kindness.
③ He is known as his kindness.
④ He is known by his kindness.

---

## 정답 + 해설

### 1. ② in

interested in (고정).

### 2. ② The desk is made of wood.

나무가 보이는 변형 → made of.

### 3. ① The bottle is filled by water.

filled with (고정). by X.

### 4. ② of

afraid of (고정).

### 5. ② He is known for his kindness.

이유 (친절) → known for.
