## 핵심 명제

> **단수 주어 → 단수 동사. neither/either, 집합명사, 가까운 주어 룰을 외워라.**

일치 함정 7가지가 시험에 모두 나온다.

---

## 설명

### 기본 — 주어 단복수에 동사 일치

```
✓ He plays soccer. (단수 + s)
✓ They play soccer. (복수)
✓ The book is on the table.
✓ The books are on the table.
```

### 함정 1 — 주어와 동사 사이 수식어구 무시

수식어가 길어도 진짜 주어로 일치.

```
✓ The boy with two dogs is my brother.
(주어 = boy 단수 → is)

✓ The students who are in this room are mine.
(주어 = students 복수 → are)
```

### 함정 2 — 집합명사 (family / team / class)

| 의미 | 동사 |
|---|---|
| 한 덩어리로 | 단수 |
| 구성원 각자 | 복수 (영국식) |

```
✓ My family is large. (한 덩어리)
✓ My family are doctors. (각자 — 영국식 빈번)
```

미국 영어는 단수가 흔함. 시험에선 보통 단수 답.

### 함정 3 — 상관접속사 — 가까운 주어 룰

| 표현 | 일치 |
|---|---|
| both A and B | **항상 복수** |
| either A or B | B 에 일치 |
| neither A nor B | B 에 일치 |
| not only A but also B | B 에 일치 |
| A as well as B | **A 에 일치** ⭐ |

```
✓ Both my brother and I like sports. (복수)
✓ Either you or he is wrong. (he 에 일치 → is)
✓ Neither he nor I am happy. (I 에 일치 → am)
✓ Not only Tom but also his friends are coming. (friends 복수)
✓ He as well as I am right. (he 에 일치 → is) — A 가 기준
```

`as well as` 는 다른 룰 (A 가 기준) — 시험 함정.

### 함정 4 — every / each / no + 명사 → 항상 단수

```
✓ Every student has a book. (every + 단수 + 단수동사)
✓ Each of us has a book.
✓ No man is perfect.
```

### 함정 5 — neither / either + of + 복수 → 단수

```
✓ Neither of them is here. (둘 다 단수 동사)
✓ Either of the books is fine.
```

### 함정 6 — 일부 명사 단복수 함정

| 명사 | 동사 |
|---|---|
| news | **단수** |
| mathematics, physics, economics | 단수 |
| the United States | 단수 |
| pants, glasses, scissors | **복수** |
| police | 복수 |
| people | 복수 |

```
✓ The news is shocking.
✓ Mathematics is hard.
✓ The United States is a big country.
✓ My pants are dirty.
✓ The police are coming.
✓ Many people are here.
```

### 함정 7 — 거리/시간/돈 단위 — 단수

```
✓ Ten miles is a long distance. (한 묶음)
✓ Five years is a long time.
✓ Ten dollars is enough.
```

### 함정 8 — there is / there are

진짜 주어는 be 동사 뒤. 그것에 일치.

```
✓ There is a book on the table.
✓ There are many books on the table.
✗ There is many books.
```

---

## 대표 문장 (외우기)

> **Either you or he is wrong, but the news of the accident is shocking.**
>
> 너 아니면 그가 틀렸다. 그런데 그 사고의 뉴스는 충격적이다.

`Either ~ or he is` (가까운 주어 he) + `news is` (news 단수). 두 함정.

## 실전 문제 5개

### 문제 1.
어법상 옳은 것은?

① The boy with two dogs are my brother.
② The boy with two dogs is my brother.
③ The boy with two dog are my brother.
④ The boy with two dogs were my brother.

### 문제 2.
다음 빈칸에 알맞은 것은?

`Neither he nor I _____ tired.`

① is  ② am  ③ are  ④ be

### 문제 3.
다음 중 어법상 **틀린** 문장은?

① The news are shocking.
② Mathematics is my favorite subject.
③ Many people are coming.
④ My pants are dirty.

### 문제 4.
빈칸에 들어갈 적절한 것은?

`Every student in this class _____ a book.`

① have  ② has  ③ are having  ④ have got

### 문제 5.
다음 우리말을 영어로 옮길 때 가장 적절한 것은?

> 톰뿐만 아니라 그의 친구들도 오고 있다.

① Not only Tom but also his friends is coming.
② Not only Tom but also his friends are coming.
③ Not only Tom but also his friends were coming.
④ Not only Tom but also his friends has come.

---

## 정답 + 해설

### 1. ② The boy with two dogs is my brother.

진짜 주어 boy 단수 → is.

### 2. ② am

Neither A nor B → B 에 일치. B = I → am.

### 3. ① The news are shocking.

news 는 -s 형이지만 단수 → `is shocking`.

### 4. ② has

every + 단수 → 단수 동사 has.

### 5. ② Not only Tom but also his friends are coming.

Not only A but also B → B 에 일치. B = friends 복수 → are.
