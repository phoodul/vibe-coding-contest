## 핵심 명제

> **4형식은 수동태 2개 가능 (IO 또는 DO 가 주어). DO 가 주어면 IO 앞에 to/for 전치사.**

핵심: 직접목적어 (사물) 가 주어가 되면 간접목적어 (사람) 앞에 전치사가 붙는다.

---

## 설명

### 4형식 = 두 개의 수동태 가능

`She gave me a book.` (4형식 — me=IO, a book=DO)

수동태 두 형태:

```
능동: She gave me a book.

수동 A (IO 가 주어):
✓ I was given a book by her.

수동 B (DO 가 주어):
✓ A book was given to me by her.
```

### 두 수동태 모두 가능한 동사

`give, send, show, tell, teach, lend, write` 등 대부분의 4형식 동사.

```
능동: He sent me an email.
A: I was sent an email by him.
B: An email was sent to me by him.
```

### B 형식의 전치사 — 동사별로 to / for / of

(2-4 `sentence-4` 에서 다룸 — 같은 룰)

| to 그룹 | for 그룹 | of 그룹 |
|---|---|---|
| give, send, show, tell, teach, lend, pay, write, hand | buy, make, get, find, cook, order, choose, leave | ask, inquire, beg |

수동태 B 형식에서도 같은 전치사.

```
능동: She bought him a gift.
A: He was bought a gift. (어색 — buy 류는 A 형식 잘 안 씀)
B: ✓ A gift was bought for him. (정상)
```

### buy / make / cook / get 류 — DO 가 주어인 B 형식만 자연

```
능동: My mom made me a cake.
A: ✗ I was made a cake. (어색 — make 의 사역 의미와 혼동)
B: ✓ A cake was made for me.
```

### 시험에 자주 나오는 패턴

**give 류** — A·B 둘 다 자연:
```
He gave me a book.
→ I was given a book.
→ A book was given to me.
```

**buy 류** — B 형식만 자연:
```
He bought me a present.
→ A present was bought for me. (B만 OK)
```

**ask** — of 전치사:
```
He asked me a question.
→ I was asked a question. (A — 자연)
→ A question was asked of me. (B — 격식)
```

### 부정사·that 절도 가능

```
능동: He asked me to help.
수동: I was asked to help (by him). (사역 동사처럼 to 부활 — 5-5)

능동: They told me that he was sick.
수동: I was told that he was sick.
```

### 수동태 부적합한 동사

| 동사 | 이유 |
|---|---|
| have (소유) | 수동 X |
| resemble | 수동 X (자체 의미) |
| cost / take (4형식) | 수동 어색 |

```
✗ A car is had by me. → ✓ I have a car.
✗ Her father is resembled by her. → ✓ She resembles her father.
✗ 10 dollars is cost by it to me. → ✓ It cost me 10 dollars.
```

## 대표 문장 (외우기)

> **A book was given to me by her, and a cake was made for me by my mom.**
>
> 그녀에 의해 책 한 권이 내게 주어졌고, 엄마에 의해 케이크가 만들어졌다.

`given to me` (give → to) + `made for me` (make → for). 4형식 B 수동의 두 전치사.

## 실전 문제 5개

### 문제 1.
다음 능동 문장의 수동태 (DO 주어) 로 알맞은 것은?

`She sent him a letter.`

① He was sent a letter.
② A letter was sent to him.
③ A letter was sent for him.
④ A letter sent to him.

### 문제 2.
어법상 옳은 것은?

① A present was bought to me.
② A present was bought for me.
③ A present was bought of me.
④ A present was bought by me to my mom.

### 문제 3.
다음 빈칸에 알맞은 것은?

`A book was given _____ me by my friend.`

① to  ② for  ③ of  ④ at

### 문제 4.
다음 중 자연스럽지 **않은** 수동태는?

① I was given a present.
② A present was given to me.
③ A cake was made for me.
④ I was bought a present.

### 문제 5.
다음 우리말을 영어로 옮길 때 가장 적절한 것은?

> 그녀에게 질문이 던져졌다 (격식).

① A question was asked to her.
② A question was asked for her.
③ A question was asked of her.
④ A question was asked by her.

---

## 정답 + 해설

### 1. ② A letter was sent to him.

send 는 to 그룹.

### 2. ② A present was bought for me.

buy 는 for 그룹.

### 3. ① to

give 는 to 그룹.

### 4. ④ I was bought a present.

buy 류는 IO 가 주어인 A 형식 어색. B (`A present was bought for me`) 만 자연.

### 5. ③ A question was asked of her.

ask 는 of 그룹 (격식).
