## 핵심 명제

> **wh-의문문 = ask + wh- + 평서문 어순. yes/no 의문문 = ask if/whether + 평서문 어순.**

도치 풀고 평서문 어순으로.

---

## 설명

### wh-의문문 — 의문사 유지 + 평서문 어순

```
직접: He asked, "What do you want?"
간접: He asked what I wanted.
(do 빠지고, want → wanted, 어순 평서문으로)

직접: She asked, "Where is your father?"
간접: She asked where my father was.
```

### yes/no 의문문 — if 또는 whether

```
직접: He asked, "Do you like coffee?"
간접: He asked if I liked coffee.
(또는: He asked whether I liked coffee.)

직접: She asked, "Are you tired?"
간접: She asked if I was tired.
```

`if` 와 `whether` 동의어. `whether` 가 약간 격식.

### 변환 단계

1. **say/tell → ask/inquire**
2. **따옴표 제거** + **wh-** (또는 if/whether)
3. **도치 풀기** — 평서문 어순
4. **시제 backshift**
5. **인칭·시간·장소 변환**

### 도치 풀기 — 핵심

의문문은 `do/does/did/be/have/can` + 주어 + 동사 형태. 간접화법에선 평서문 어순.

```
직접: "Do you have time?" (do + 주어 + 동사)
간접: ... if I had time. (주어 + 동사, do 사라짐)

직접: "Are you a student?"
간접: ... if I was a student.

직접: "When will he arrive?"
간접: ... when he would arrive. (when + 주어 + 동사)
```

### ask + 사람 목적어 가능

```
✓ He asked me, "Do you have time?"
→ He asked me if I had time.

✓ He asked, "Where do you live?"
→ He asked where I lived.
```

`ask` 는 사람 목적어 있어도 없어도 OK.

### 부탁·명령 화법 — ask/tell + O + to V

```
직접: She said to me, "Please help me."
간접: She asked me to help her.

직접: He said, "Don't be late."
간접: He told me not to be late.
```

부정 명령은 `not + to V`.

| 직접 동사 | 간접화법 동사 |
|---|---|
| 부탁 | ask |
| 명령 | tell / order |
| 충고 | advise |
| 제안 | suggest |
| 경고 | warn |

### 감탄문 — say + how/what + ...

```
직접: She said, "How beautiful the sunset is!"
간접: She said (exclaimed) how beautiful the sunset was.
```

### 자주 시험 패턴 — 변환 후 다시 평서문

```
직접: He said, "I am happy."
→ He said that he was happy.

직접: He asked, "Are you happy?"
→ He asked if I was happy.

직접: He said, "Be quiet."
→ He told me to be quiet.

직접: He said, "Don't go."
→ He told me not to go.
```

### 실수 가장 잦은 부분

```
✗ He asked me where do I live.
✓ He asked me where I lived.

✗ He asked me if did I have time.
✓ He asked me if I had time.

✗ He told me to don't be late.
✓ He told me not to be late.
```

도치 풀기 + do 제거 + 부정 위치.

## 대표 문장 (외우기)

> **He asked me where I lived, and told me to come early.**
>
> 그는 내가 어디 사는지 물었고, 일찍 오라고 했다.

`asked me where I lived` (wh-의문문 간접) + `told me to come early` (명령 → tell + to V). 두 핵심.

## 실전 문제 5개

### 문제 1.
다음 직접화법을 간접화법으로 가장 자연스럽게 바꾼 것은?

`He asked, "What do you want?"`

① He asked what do I want.
② He asked what I want.
③ He asked what I wanted.
④ He asked what did I want.

### 문제 2.
어법상 옳은 것은?

① She asked me if I am tired.
② She asked me if I was tired.
③ She asked me whether am I tired.
④ She asked me whether did I tired.

### 문제 3.
다음 직접화법을 간접화법으로 가장 자연스럽게 바꾼 것은?

`He said to me, "Please help me."`

① He told me please help him.
② He told me to please help him.
③ He asked me to help him.
④ He said to help me him.

### 문제 4.
다음 직접 명령문을 간접화법으로 바꿀 때 가장 자연스러운 것은?

`She said, "Don't be late."`

① She told me to not be late.
② She told me don't be late.
③ She told me not to be late.
④ She told me to be not late.

### 문제 5.
다음 우리말을 영어로 옮길 때 가장 적절한 것은?

> 그는 내게 어디 사느냐고 물었다.

① He asked me where do I live.
② He asked me where I live.
③ He asked me where I lived.
④ He asked me where did I lived.

---

## 정답 + 해설

### 1. ③ He asked what I wanted.

wh- + 평서문 어순 + backshift.

### 2. ② She asked me if I was tired.

if + 평서문 + backshift.

### 3. ③ He asked me to help him.

부탁 → ask + O + to V.

### 4. ③ She told me not to be late.

부정 명령 → not + to V (not 위치는 to 앞).

### 5. ③ He asked me where I lived.

wh- + 평서문 + backshift (live → lived).
