## 핵심 명제

> **목적어 없는 자동사 = 수동태 불가. happen/occur/disappear/arrive/exist/die.**

수동태의 가장 큰 함정.

---

## 설명

수동태는 능동의 **목적어를 주어로** 만드는 구조. 목적어가 없는 자동사는 수동태로 못 변환.

### 자주 시험에 나오는 자동사 8 대장

| 자동사 | 의미 |
|---|---|
| happen | 일어나다 |
| occur | 발생하다 |
| take place | 열리다 |
| arrive | 도착하다 |
| exist | 존재하다 |
| disappear | 사라지다 |
| appear | 나타나다 |
| die | 죽다 |

```
✗ The accident was happened.
✓ The accident happened.

✗ He was died yesterday.
✓ He died yesterday.

✗ The Olympics will be taken place in 2030.
✓ The Olympics will take place in 2030.
```

### 자동사 + 전치사 — "구동사" 는 수동 가능

자동사라도 전치사와 결합해 의미상 타동사가 되면 수동 가능.

| 구동사 | 의미 | 수동 |
|---|---|---|
| look at | 보다 | be looked at |
| listen to | 듣다 | be listened to |
| laugh at | 비웃다 | be laughed at |
| take care of | 돌보다 | be taken care of |
| make use of | 이용하다 | be made use of |
| pay attention to | 주의하다 | be paid attention to |
| put up with | 참다 | be put up with |
| run over | (차로) 치다 | be run over |

```
능동: She took care of the baby.
수동: ✓ The baby was taken care of by her.

능동: They laughed at him.
수동: ✓ He was laughed at by them.
```

전치사를 빠뜨리지 말 것:

```
✗ The baby was taken care.
✓ The baby was taken care of.
```

### 양쪽 의미 다른 동사 — 자동사 vs 타동사

같은 동사가 자동사·타동사로 쓰일 수 있음.

| 동사 | 자동사 의미 (수동 X) | 타동사 의미 (수동 O) |
|---|---|---|
| run | 달리다 | 운영하다 |
| grow | 자라다 | 재배하다 |
| break | 부서지다 | 부수다 |

```
✗ He was ran fast. (자동사)
✓ He ran fast.
✓ The company is run by him. (타동사 — 운영하다)
```

### 자주 시험에 나오는 함정 동사

| 동사 | 자동사인가 |
|---|---|
| happen | 자동사 — 수동 X |
| occur | 자동사 — 수동 X |
| die | 자동사 — 수동 X |
| arise | 자동사 — 수동 X |
| seem / appear | 자동사 (보이다) — 수동 X |
| consist of | 자동사 — 수동 X |
| belong to | 자동사 — 수동 X |
| resemble | **타동사**지만 자체 의미상 수동 X |

```
✗ This bag is belonged to me.
✓ This bag belongs to me.

✗ Her father is resembled by her.
✓ She resembles her father.
```

### 자동사 + 전치사 + 명사 — 분사구문에서 분사 수동

```
✓ He was looked at strangely. (look at 의 수동)
✓ The proposal was agreed to. (agree to)
```

전치사 위치는 마지막 (사라지지 않음).

## 대표 문장 (외우기)

> **The accident happened yesterday, but the issue was discussed by the team.**
>
> 어제 사고가 일어났다. 그러나 그 이슈는 팀에 의해 논의되었다.

`accident happened` (자동사 — 수동 X) + `was discussed` (타동사 — 수동 O). 자/타동사 차이.

---

## 실전 문제 5개

### 문제 1.
어법상 옳은 것은?

① The accident was happened last night.
② The accident happened last night.
③ The accident is happened last night.
④ The accident has been happened last night.

### 문제 2.
다음 능동 문장의 수동태로 알맞은 것은?

`They laughed at him.`

① He was laughed at by them.
② He was laughed by them.
③ He was laughing at by them.
④ At him was laughed.

### 문제 3.
다음 중 어법상 **틀린** 문장은?

① The baby is taken care of by her grandma.
② The Olympics will take place next month.
③ This book is belonged to my brother.
④ He resembles his father.

### 문제 4.
빈칸에 들어갈 적절한 것은?

`The proposal was agreed _____ at the meeting.`

① by  ② to  ③ on  ④ for

### 문제 5.
다음 우리말을 영어로 옮길 때 가장 적절한 것은?

> 그는 갑자기 사라졌다.

① He was suddenly disappeared.
② He suddenly disappeared.
③ He has been suddenly disappeared.
④ He is suddenly disappeared.

---

## 정답 + 해설

### 1. ② The accident happened last night.

happen 자동사 → 수동 X.

### 2. ① He was laughed at by them.

laugh at 구동사 수동. at 빠지면 X.

### 3. ③ This book is belonged to my brother.

belong 자동사 → 수동 X. → `This book belongs to my brother.`

### 4. ② to

agree to (구동사). 수동에서도 to 유지.

### 5. ② He suddenly disappeared.

disappear 자동사 → 수동 X.
