## 핵심 명제

> **선행사가 사람 + 관계절에서 주어 = who, 목적어 = whom (구어 who).**

관계대명사는 두 문장을 하나로 묶는 다리.

---

## 설명

### 관계대명사란

두 문장에서 같은 사람·사물을 가리키는 부분을 묶어 한 문장으로.

```
[1] I know the boy. + [2] The boy is wearing a red shirt.
→ I know the boy who is wearing a red shirt.
```

`who` 가 두 문장을 연결 + the boy 의 자리를 대신.

### 격에 따른 선택 — 사람 선행사

| 격 | 형태 |
|---|---|
| 주격 (관계절 안에서 주어) | who (또는 that) |
| 목적격 (관계절 안에서 목적어) | whom (또는 who, that, 생략) |
| 소유격 | whose |

### 주격 who — 관계절에서 주어 역할

```
✓ The boy who is standing there is my brother.
(who is standing — boy 가 standing 의 주어)

✓ I know a man who can speak 5 languages.
```

### 목적격 whom — 관계절에서 목적어

```
✓ The boy whom I met yesterday is my brother.
(whom I met — boy 가 met 의 목적어)

✓ The man whom you saw is a teacher.
```

구어에선 whom 대신 **who** 또는 **that** 또는 **생략**:

```
✓ The man who you saw is a teacher. (구어)
✓ The man that you saw is a teacher. (구어)
✓ The man you saw is a teacher. (생략 — 가장 흔함)
```

### 전치사 + whom — 격식 형태

전치사가 관계절 마지막에 있던 것을 앞으로 옮긴 격식 표현.

```
✓ The man with whom I talked is my uncle. (격식)
✓ The man (whom/who/that) I talked with is my uncle. (구어)
```

전치사 + who/that 은 X — 전치사 직후엔 whom 만.

```
✗ The man with who I talked
✗ The man with that I talked
✓ The man with whom I talked
```

### 주격 vs 목적격 — 구별법

관계절 안에 **동사 바로 다음에 빈자리** 가 있으면 → 주격 (관계대명사 자체가 그 주어).

```
The girl ___ is studying.  → 주격 (___가 주어)
The girl ___ I met.        → 목적격 (I 가 주어, ___은 met 의 목적어)
```

### 자주 시험에 나오는 패턴

```
✓ This is the doctor who treated me. (who 주격)
✓ This is the doctor (whom/that/_) I trust. (목적격)
✓ This is the doctor with whom I work. (전치사 + whom)
```

### 소유격 whose — 8-3 에서 자세히

```
✓ The boy whose father is a doctor lives next door.
(아버지가 의사인 그 소년)
```

## 대표 문장 (외우기)

> **The teacher who taught us is the same one whom we respect.**
>
> 우리를 가르치셨던 그 선생님이 우리가 존경하는 바로 그 분이다.

`who taught` (주격) + `whom we respect` (목적격). 두 격이 한 문장에.

## 실전 문제 5개

### 문제 1.
다음 빈칸에 알맞은 것은?

`The boy _____ is sitting next to me is my cousin.`

① who  ② whom  ③ whose  ④ which

### 문제 2.
어법상 옳은 것은?

① The man whom is wearing a hat is my uncle.
② The man who is wearing a hat is my uncle.
③ The man who I saw he is a doctor.
④ The man whom is a doctor came in.

### 문제 3.
다음 중 어법상 **틀린** 문장은?

① The student who passed the exam was happy.
② The friend whom I trust is honest.
③ The girl that I met yesterday called me.
④ The teacher with that I talked is famous.

### 문제 4.
다음 두 문장을 한 문장으로 가장 자연스럽게 합친 것은?

`I met a girl. + The girl was very kind.`

① I met a girl whom was very kind.
② I met a girl who was very kind.
③ I met a girl whose was very kind.
④ I met a girl which was very kind.

### 문제 5.
다음 빈칸에 알맞은 것은?

`The man _____ I work is very nice.`

① with whom  ② whom with  ③ with that  ④ with who

---

## 정답 + 해설

### 1. ① who

관계절에서 주어 → who 주격. ② whom 목적격. ③ 소유격. ④ 사물 선행사용.

### 2. ② The man who is wearing a hat is my uncle.

주격 who. ① whom + is 주어 자리 X. ③ who I saw he 중복. ④ whom + is X.

### 3. ④ The teacher with that I talked is famous.

전치사 + that X. → `with whom` 또는 `who I talked with`.

### 4. ② I met a girl who was very kind.

선행사 girl(사람) + 주격 → who. ④ which 는 사물.

### 5. ① with whom

전치사 + whom 만 가능.
