## 핵심 명제

> **본동사보다 더 이전 시점이면 having + p.p. (완료분사) 사용.**

분사구문에서 시제 차이를 표시하는 유일한 방법.

---

## 설명

### 단순분사 vs 완료분사

| 형태 | 시점 |
|---|---|
| **V-ing** (단순) | 본동사와 같은 시점 |
| **having + p.p.** (완료) | 본동사보다 더 이전 |

```
✓ Knowing the answer, he raised his hand.
(답을 안다 — 손을 들었다, 같은 시점)

✓ Having known the answer, he raised his hand.
(답을 이미 알고 있었다 — 그 후 손을 들었다)
```

### 부사절로 풀어보기

```
완료분사: Having finished my homework, I went out.
부사절: After I had finished my homework, I went out.
(= 숙제 끝난 후 → 나갔다, 두 시점 차이)

완료분사: Having lived in Seoul for 10 years, he knows it well.
부사절: Because he has lived in Seoul for 10 years, he knows it well.
```

### 단순분사 + 단순본동사 — 거의 동시

```
✓ Hearing the news, she cried. (소식 듣고 울었다 — 거의 동시)
```

### 완료분사 + 단순본동사 — 명확히 시제 차이

```
✓ Having heard the news, she felt better.
(소식 들은 후 — 좀 시간 지나 — 기분 나아짐)
```

### 수동 형태 — Having been + p.p.

```
✓ Having been told the truth, she felt relieved.
(진실을 들은 후, 안도했다)

✓ Having been chosen as the leader, he felt responsible.
(리더로 뽑힌 후, 책임감 느꼈다)
```

### 부정 — Not having + p.p.

```
✓ Not having met her before, I didn't recognize her.
(전에 만난 적 없어서, 알아보지 못했다)

✓ Not having finished my homework, I couldn't go out.
```

### 자주 쓰이는 패턴

`Having + p.p.` 는 **이유** 의미가 가장 흔함.

```
✓ Having lived abroad, she speaks fluent English. (이유)
✓ Having received the prize, he felt proud. (시간 + 결과)
✓ Having missed the bus, I had to walk. (이유)
```

### 단순 분사 vs 완료 분사 — 비교

```
✗ Living in Seoul for 10 years, he knows it well. (의미상 어색 — 같은 시점이면 현재진행)
✓ Having lived in Seoul for 10 years, he knows it well. (10년 살아 옴 → 안다)

✗ Eating dinner, I did the dishes. (밥 먹으며 설거지 — 동시 부자연)
✓ Having eaten dinner, I did the dishes. (밥 먹은 후 설거지 — 자연)
```

### 단순분사로도 OK 한 케이스

본동사 시점에 완료된 결과 상태가 명확하면 단순분사로도 OK.

```
✓ Born in Korea, he speaks Korean. (한국에서 태어났다 — 본 시점)
✓ Made of wood, the chair is light. (나무로 만들어졌다)
```

수동의 경우 `(Having been) Born` 에서 `Having been` 생략.

---

## 대표 문장 (외우기)

> **Having finished my homework, I went out to play.**
>
> 숙제를 다 끝낸 후, 나는 놀러 나갔다.

`Having finished` (완료분사 — 본동사 went 보다 더 이전). 시간 차이 명시의 대표.

## 실전 문제 5개

### 문제 1.
다음 두 문장의 의미가 같도록 빈칸에 알맞은 것은?

`After I had eaten lunch, I went to the library.`
= `_____ lunch, I went to the library.`

① Eating  ② Eaten  ③ Having eaten  ④ Have eaten

### 문제 2.
어법상 옳은 것은?

① Lived in Paris for 5 years, she speaks French.
② Living in Paris for 5 years, she speaks French.
③ Having lived in Paris for 5 years, she speaks French.
④ To live in Paris for 5 years, she speaks French.

### 문제 3.
다음 중 어법상 **틀린** 문장은?

① Having been told the news, she felt sad.
② Having finished work, he went home.
③ Not having seen him before, I didn't know him.
④ Have been chosen as leader, he felt proud.

### 문제 4.
빈칸에 들어갈 적절한 것은?

`_____ in 1990, he is 35 years old now.`

① Be born  ② Born  ③ Bear  ④ Bore

### 문제 5.
다음 우리말을 영어로 옮길 때 가장 적절한 것은?

> 그는 시험에 떨어진 후, 다시 공부를 시작했다.

① Failing the exam, he started studying again.
② Having failed the exam, he started studying again.
③ Failed the exam, he started studying again.
④ Have failed the exam, he started studying again.

## 정답 + 해설

### 1. ③ Having eaten

본동사(went 과거) 보다 더 이전 (had eaten 과거완료) → 완료분사.

### 2. ③ Having lived in Paris for 5 years, she speaks French.

5년 살아온 결과 → 현재 영어 잘함 = 시제 차이 → 완료분사. ① -ed X. ② 단순분사 + for 5 years 어색.

### 3. ④ Have been chosen as leader, he felt proud.

`Having been + p.p.` 가 정상. → `Having been chosen as leader, he felt proud.` Have 형태 X.

### 4. ② Born

수동 + (Having been) Born = 보통 Born 단독으로. ① Be born X. ③ ④ 동사 형태 X.

### 5. ② Having failed the exam, he started studying again.

떨어진 후 → 다시 공부 = 시간 차이 명확 → 완료분사. ① 단순분사는 동시 어감. ③ -ed 단독 X. ④ Have X.
