## 핵심 명제

> **if 생략 + 동사 앞으로 도치 = 격식 가정법. Were I / Had I / Should I.**

격식 글·연설에서 자주 등장.

---

## 설명

### 도치 가능한 3가지 형태

if 절에서 다음 3 동사가 있을 때, **if 생략 + 동사 + 주어** 도치 가능.

| 원래 형태 | 도치 형태 |
|---|---|
| If + 주어 + were | Were + 주어 |
| If + 주어 + had + p.p. | Had + 주어 + p.p. |
| If + 주어 + should + V | Should + 주어 + V |

### 1) Were + 주어 (가정법 과거)

```
원래: If I were you, I wouldn't do that.
도치: Were I you, I wouldn't do that.

원래: If she were here, she would help.
도치: Were she here, she would help.
```

### 2) Had + 주어 + p.p. (가정법 과거완료)

```
원래: If I had known, I would have told you.
도치: Had I known, I would have told you.

원래: If you had been there, you would have seen it.
도치: Had you been there, you would have seen it.
```

가장 자주 출제되는 패턴.

### 3) Should + 주어 + V (가정법 미래·격식)

`should` 는 "혹시 ~한다면" 미약한 가정.

```
원래: If you should have any questions, please contact me.
도치: Should you have any questions, please contact me.
```

격식 비즈니스 메일 단골.

### 도치형의 부정

부정형은 not 위치를 그대로.

```
✓ Had I not been late, I wouldn't have missed the bus.
= If I had not been late, ...

✓ Were it not for your help, I would fail.
= If it were not for your help, ... (= Without your help)
```

### Were it not for / Had it not been for — 관용

| 표현 | 의미 |
|---|---|
| **Were it not for** ~ | ~이 없다면 (가정법 과거) |
| **Had it not been for** ~ | ~이 없었다면 (가정법 과거완료) |
| (= If it were not for / If it had not been for) | (도치 안 한 형태) |
| (= But for / Without) | 더 짧은 표현 |

```
✓ Were it not for water, we would die. (물 없으면)
✓ Had it not been for your advice, I would have failed. (네 충고 없었더라면)
✓ But for your help, I would be lost. (= Without)
```

### 도치 vs 일반 — 의미는 같음, 격식만 다름

```
일상: If I were you, ...
격식: Were I you, ...
```

뜻 동일. 격식·연설·법률·비즈니스에 도치 자주.

### 도치의 다른 패턴 — 단원 13 에서

가정법 외 도치 (Never, Hardly, Not only) 는 단원 13 `inversion-negative` 에서.

---

## 대표 문장 (외우기)

> **Had I known earlier, I would have decided differently. — Were it not for your help, I would fail.**
>
> 더 일찍 알았더라면 다르게 결정했을 텐데. 네 도움이 없으면 나는 실패할 것이다.

`Had I known` (도치 — 가정법 과거완료) + `Were it not for` (관용 도치). 두 핵심 패턴.

## 실전 문제 5개

### 문제 1.
다음 두 문장의 의미가 같도록 빈칸에 알맞은 것은?

`If I had known the truth, I would have told you.`
= `_____ the truth, I would have told you.`

① Had I known  ② Was I known  ③ I had known  ④ Knew I

### 문제 2.
어법상 옳은 것은?

① Were I you, I will not do that.
② Were I you, I would not do that.
③ Was I you, I would not do that.
④ Were I you, I would not did that.

### 문제 3.
다음 빈칸에 알맞은 것은?

`_____ you have any questions, please contact me.`

① If  ② Were  ③ Should  ④ Had

### 문제 4.
다음 중 어법상 **틀린** 문장은?

① Had it not been for your help, I would have failed.
② Were it not for water, we would die.
③ Had I not been late, I would have caught the train.
④ Were I had time, I would help you.

### 문제 5.
다음 우리말을 영어로 옮길 때 가장 적절한 것은?

> 그의 충고가 없었다면, 나는 실패했을 것이다.

① Was it not for his advice, I would fail.
② Had it not been for his advice, I would have failed.
③ Were not for his advice, I would have failed.
④ If not been for his advice, I would have failed.

---

## 정답 + 해설

### 1. ① Had I known

가정법 과거완료 도치 = Had + 주어 + p.p.

### 2. ② Were I you, I would not do that.

가정법 과거 도치 + would + 원형. ① will X. ③ Was I you 비표준 (구어 OK). ④ did X.

### 3. ③ Should

격식 가정 = Should + 주어 + V. (= If you should have)

### 4. ④ Were I had time, I would help you.

`Were I` 다음에 had time 못 옴. → `Had I time, I would help` (격식) 또는 `If I had time, ...`.

### 5. ② Had it not been for his advice, I would have failed.

과거 충고 없었더라면 → had not been for + would have p.p.
