## 핵심 명제

> **부정·제한 부사가 문두에 오면 의문문 어순 (조동사 + 주어). 강조 효과.**

5가지 패턴이 시험에 모두 등장.

---

## 설명

### 부정 부사 문두 도치 — 5 대장

| 부사 | 의미 |
|---|---|
| Never | 결코 ~ 아닌 |
| Rarely / Seldom | 거의 ~ 아닌 |
| Hardly / Scarcely | 거의 ~ 아닌 |
| Not only | ~뿐 아니라 |
| No sooner | ~하자마자 |
| Little | 거의 ~ 아닌 (사고 동사와) |
| Only when/after/then | ~할 때 비로소 |
| Under no circumstances | 어떤 일이 있어도 |

이 부사들이 문두에 오면 **주어·동사 도치** (의문문 어순).

### 도치 형태 — 의문문 어순

```
일반: I have never seen such beauty.
도치: Never have I seen such beauty.

일반: He rarely visits us.
도치: Rarely does he visit us.

일반: She had hardly arrived when the phone rang.
도치: Hardly had she arrived when the phone rang.
```

조동사 (have/has/had/do/does/did/will/can 등) 가 주어 앞으로.

### 도치 변환 — be 동사·일반동사·조동사

```
be 동사:
일반: He was never late.
도치: Never was he late.

조동사 있는 경우:
일반: I have never seen it.
도치: Never have I seen it.

일반동사 (do/does/did 추가):
일반: She rarely smiles.
도치: Rarely does she smile.
(s 빠지고 does 추가)

일반: He never told me.
도치: Never did he tell me.
(told → tell + did)
```

### Not only A but also B — 도치

```
일반: He is not only smart but also kind.
도치: Not only is he smart but also kind.

일반: She not only sings but also dances.
도치: Not only does she sing but also dances.
```

A 와 B 가 절일 땐 첫 절만 도치.

### No sooner ~ than — ~하자마자

```
✓ No sooner had I arrived than the rain started.
(도착하자마자 비가 시작됐다)

= Hardly had I arrived when the rain started.
= I had hardly arrived when the rain started. (도치 안 함)
= As soon as I arrived, the rain started. (단순)
```

`No sooner ~ than` 과 `Hardly ~ when` 짝꿍. 자주 시험 출제.

### Only when / after / if — 도치

```
일반: I realized the truth only then.
도치: Only then did I realize the truth.

일반: You can succeed only if you try.
도치: Only if you try can you succeed. (when 절 끝나고 도치)
```

### Under no circumstances — 격식

```
✓ Under no circumstances should you give up.
(어떤 상황에서도 포기해선 안 된다)
```

격식 글·연설.

### Little — 사고 동사와

`little` 이 think/dream/expect 같은 사고 동사와 함께 문두 도치.

```
✓ Little did I know what would happen.
(나는 무슨 일이 일어날지 거의 몰랐다)

✓ Little did she dream of becoming a star.
```

### 가정법 도치 — 9-6 에서 다룸

```
✓ Were I rich, I would travel.
✓ Had I known, I would have told you.
```

같은 도치 구조지만 가정법 if 생략에 의한 것.

## 대표 문장 (외우기)

> **Never have I seen such a beautiful sunset, and rarely does she go out at night.**
>
> 그렇게 아름다운 일몰을 본 적이 없다. 그리고 그녀는 밤에 거의 외출하지 않는다.

`Never have I seen` (현재완료 도치) + `rarely does she go out` (일반동사 + does 도치). 두 패턴.

---

## 실전 문제 5개

### 문제 1.
다음 두 문장의 의미가 같도록 빈칸에 알맞은 것은?

`I have never seen such beauty.`
= `_____ such beauty.`

① Never I have seen
② Never have I seen
③ Never did I see
④ Have never I seen

### 문제 2.
어법상 옳은 것은?

① Rarely she does smile.
② Rarely does she smile.
③ Rarely she smiles.
④ Rarely smiles she.

### 문제 3.
다음 두 문장의 의미가 같은 것은?

`No sooner had I arrived than the rain started.`

① I arrived after the rain started.
② As soon as I arrived, the rain started.
③ When the rain started, I had already left.
④ The rain started long before I arrived.

### 문제 4.
다음 빈칸에 알맞은 것은?

`Not only _____ smart, but he is also kind.`

① he is  ② is he  ③ does he  ④ has he

### 문제 5.
다음 우리말을 영어로 옮길 때 가장 적절한 것은?

> 어떤 상황에서도 너는 포기해서는 안 된다.

① Under no circumstances you should give up.
② Under no circumstances should you give up.
③ Under no circumstances do give up you.
④ Under no circumstances are you give up.

---

## 정답 + 해설

### 1. ② Never have I seen

부정 부사 문두 → 도치 (조동사 have + 주어 I).

### 2. ② Rarely does she smile.

일반동사 → does 추가 + 주어 + 원형. ① 어순 X. ③ 도치 X. ④ 도치 형태 X.

### 3. ② As soon as I arrived, the rain started.

No sooner ~ than = Hardly ~ when = As soon as.

### 4. ② is he

Not only + be 동사 도치 (is he + 형용사).

### 5. ② Under no circumstances should you give up.

`Under no circumstances` 부정 부사구 → 도치 (조동사 should + 주어 you).
