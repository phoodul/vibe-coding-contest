## 핵심 명제

> **셋 이상 중 최고. 1~2 음절 -est, 3+ 음절 the most. 항상 the.**

비교급과 같은 룰 + 정관사 the.

---

## 설명

### 최상급 만들기

| 음절 | 규칙 | 예 |
|---|---|---|
| 1음절 | + est | tall → tallest |
| 1음절 -e | + st | nice → nicest |
| 1음절 단모음+자음 | 자음 중복 + est | big → biggest |
| 2음절 -y | -y → -i + est | happy → happiest |
| 3음절 이상 | most + 원급 | beautiful → most beautiful |

### 불규칙 최상급

| 원급 | 비교급 | 최상급 |
|---|---|---|
| good / well | better | best |
| bad / ill | worse | worst |
| many / much | more | most |
| little | less | least |
| far | farther / further | farthest / furthest |

### 형태 — the + 최상급

최상급 앞에 보통 **정관사 the**.

```
✓ He is the tallest in our class.
✓ This is the best book I've ever read.
✓ Mt. Everest is the highest mountain in the world.
```

### in / of — 범위 표시

| 전치사 | 범위 |
|---|---|
| in + 단수 (장소·집단) | in the world, in our class, in Korea |
| of + 복수 | of all, of the three, of his friends |

```
✓ She is the smartest in our class.
✓ He is the tallest of his brothers.
✓ This is the best of all.
```

### 강조 — much / by far / very

| 강조 | 위치 |
|---|---|
| **much / by far** | the 앞 |
| **very** | the 뒤 + 최상급 앞 (드물게) |

```
✓ This is much the best book.
✓ This is by far the best movie I've ever seen.
✓ This is the very best. (very 가 the 와 most/best 사이)
```

### one of the + 최상급 + 복수명사 — ~중 하나

```
✓ He is one of the best players in the world.
✓ This is one of the most beautiful places.
```

복수명사 (players, places) 주의. 단수 명사 X.

```
✗ He is one of the best player.
✓ He is one of the best players.
```

### 최상급 패턴 변환 — 원급·비교급으로

`the tallest in ~` 을 다른 형태로 표현 가능.

```
He is the tallest boy in the class.
= No other boy in the class is as tall as he. (원급)
= No other boy in the class is taller than he. (비교급)
= He is taller than any other boy in the class. (비교급)
```

시험에 단골 변환 패턴.

### 관용 표현

| 표현 | 의미 |
|---|---|
| at (the) most | 많아야 |
| at (the) least | 적어도 |
| at best | 잘 봐줘야 |
| do one's best | 최선을 다하다 |
| make the best of | ~를 최대한 활용하다 |

```
✓ He has at most $10. (많아야 10달러)
✓ At least try once.
✓ Do your best.
```

## 대표 문장 (외우기)

> **He is one of the best students in our class — by far the smartest.**
>
> 그는 우리 반에서 최고의 학생 중 하나다 — 단연코 가장 똑똑하다.

`one of the best students` (~중 하나, 복수) + `by far the smartest` (강조). 핵심 패턴.

---

## 실전 문제 5개

### 문제 1.
다음 빈칸에 알맞은 것은?

`This is _____ book in the library.`

① bigger  ② biggest  ③ the biggest  ④ the bigger

### 문제 2.
어법상 옳은 것은?

① He is one of the best player in the team.
② He is one of the best players in the team.
③ He is one of best players in the team.
④ He is one the best players in the team.

### 문제 3.
다음 중 어법상 **틀린** 문장은?

① He is the tallest boy in our class.
② She is the smartest of the three.
③ This is more beautiful place I've ever been.
④ Mt. Everest is the highest mountain in the world.

### 문제 4.
빈칸에 들어갈 적절한 것은?

`This is _____ the best movie I've ever seen.`

① very  ② much  ③ by far  ④ ② 와 ③ 모두 가능

### 문제 5.
다음 우리말을 영어로 옮길 때 가장 적절한 것은?

> 그녀는 우리 반에서 가장 친절하다.

① She is the kindest in our class.
② She is the most kindest in our class.
③ She is more kind in our class.
④ She is the kindest of our class.

---

## 정답 + 해설

### 1. ③ the biggest

최상급 = the + 최상급.

### 2. ② He is one of the best players in the team.

one of the 최상급 + 복수명사.

### 3. ③ This is more beautiful place I've ever been.

최상급 → `the most beautiful place I've ever been`. 또한 the 누락.

### 4. ④ ② 와 ③ 모두 가능

much / by far 둘 다 the 앞 강조.

### 5. ① She is the kindest in our class.

장소·집단 → in. of 는 복수 명사 (of all 등).
