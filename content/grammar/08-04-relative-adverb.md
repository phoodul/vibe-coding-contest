## 핵심 명제

> **관계부사 = 전치사 + 관계대명사. 장소 where, 시간 when, 이유 why, 방법 how.**

선행사 종류로 결정.

---

## 설명

### 4가지 관계부사

| 관계부사 | = 전치사 + which | 선행사 |
|---|---|---|
| where | in/at/to/on which | 장소 (the place, the city) |
| when | in/at/on which | 시간 (the day, the time, the year) |
| why | for which | 이유 (the reason) |
| how | in which | 방법 (the way) |

### where — 장소

```
✓ This is the city where I was born.
= This is the city in which I was born.
= This is the city which I was born in.
```

세 형태 다 같은 의미. 관계부사 형태가 가장 단순.

### when — 시간

```
✓ I'll never forget the day when I met you.
= I'll never forget the day on which I met you.
= I'll never forget the day (that) I met you. (생략)
```

### why — 이유

선행사 = `the reason`.

```
✓ This is the reason why I am late.
= This is the reason for which I am late. (격식)
= This is the reason (that) I am late. (생략)
```

### how — 방법 — 함정!

선행사 = `the way`. **the way 와 how 는 동시에 쓸 수 없다**.

```
✗ This is the way how I solved it.
✓ This is how I solved it.
✓ This is the way I solved it.
✓ This is the way in which I solved it.
```

`the way` 또는 `how` 둘 중 하나만. 둘 다 쓰면 X.

### 관계부사 vs 관계대명사 — 구별법

관계대명사 뒤에는 **불완전한 절** (주어·목적어 빠짐), 관계부사 뒤에는 **완전한 절**.

```
✓ The book which I read is interesting.
(I read 의 목적어 자리 빈 = 불완전 → 관계대명사 which)

✓ The room where I sleep is small.
(I sleep — 자동사 + 완전한 절 → 관계부사 where)
```

`I sleep in the room` 의 `in the room` 이 관계부사로 바뀐 것.

### 선행사 생략

선행사가 일반적인 단어면 생략 가능.

```
✓ This is (the place) where I live.
✓ Tell me (the time) when you arrive.
✓ I don't know (the reason) why he left.
```

### 관계부사 that 으로 바꾸기

관계부사는 `that` 또는 생략 가능.

```
✓ The day when I met you = The day (that) I met you
✓ The reason why he left = The reason (that) he left
```

### 자주 시험 패턴

```
✓ Seoul is the city where I was born. (장소 → where)
✓ Spring is the season when flowers bloom. (시간 → when)
✓ Tell me the reason why you are late. (이유 → why)
✓ This is how it works. (방법 → how, the way 없이)
```

---

## 대표 문장 (외우기)

> **I remember the day when we met, but I don't know the reason why she left.**
>
> 우리가 만난 날을 기억한다. 그런데 그녀가 왜 떠났는지는 모른다.

`the day when` (시간) + `the reason why` (이유). 두 관계부사 패턴.

## 실전 문제 5개

### 문제 1.
다음 빈칸에 알맞은 것은?

`This is the city _____ I was born.`

① which  ② where  ③ when  ④ why

### 문제 2.
어법상 옳은 것은?

① This is the way how I solved it.
② This is the way which I solved it.
③ This is how I solved it.
④ This is the way how it.

### 문제 3.
다음 중 어법상 **틀린** 문장은?

① I remember the day when we met.
② Tell me the reason why you cried.
③ This is the place where I live.
④ This is the room which I sleep.

### 문제 4.
빈칸에 들어갈 적절한 것은?

`I'll never forget the moment _____ I won the prize.`

① where  ② when  ③ why  ④ how

### 문제 5.
다음 두 문장을 한 문장으로 가장 자연스럽게 합친 것은?

`This is the school. + I studied at the school.`

① This is the school which I studied.
② This is the school where I studied.
③ This is the school why I studied.
④ This is the school when I studied.

---

## 정답 + 해설

### 1. ② where

장소 → where.

### 2. ③ This is how I solved it.

the way + how 동시 X. 둘 중 하나만.

### 3. ④ This is the room which I sleep.

`I sleep` 자동사 + 완전한 절 → 관계부사 where 필요. → `where I sleep` 또는 `which I sleep in`.

### 4. ② when

시간 (moment) → when.

### 5. ② This is the school where I studied.

at the school → where.
