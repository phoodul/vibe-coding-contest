## 핵심 명제

> **따옴표 → that 절. 시제·인칭·시간·장소 모두 변환.**

화법 변환은 4가지 동시 변환.

---

## 설명

### 직접화법 vs 간접화법

| 형식 | 예 |
|---|---|
| 직접화법 | He said, "I am tired." |
| 간접화법 | He said (that) he was tired. |

### 변환 4 단계

1. **따옴표 제거**, that 으로 연결 (생략 가능)
2. **시제 backshift** (12-2 / 3-10 참조)
3. **인칭 변환** (말하는 사람 시점으로)
4. **시간·장소 표현 변환**

### 인칭 변환

화자(주어) 와 청자(목적어) 시점에 맞춰 변환.

```
He said, "I love you."
→ He said (that) he loved her. (I → he, you → her)

She said to me, "You are right."
→ She told me (that) I was right. (You → I)
```

### 시간·장소 표현 변환

| 직접 | 간접 |
|---|---|
| now | then |
| today | that day |
| tomorrow | the next day / the following day |
| yesterday | the day before / the previous day |
| ago | before |
| this | that |
| these | those |
| here | there |
| last week | the previous week / the week before |
| next week | the following week |

```
He said, "I will come tomorrow."
→ He said (that) he would come the next day.
```

### say vs tell

| 동사 | 사용 |
|---|---|
| say | 직접 목적어 X (또는 to + 사람) |
| tell | 사람 목적어 O (간접 목적어) |

```
✓ He said, "..." / He said that ~. (목적어 X)
✓ He said to me, "..." (to + 사람)
✓ He told me that ~. (직접 목적어 me)
✗ He said me that ~.
✗ He told that ~.
```

`tell` 다음에 사람 목적어 필수, `say` 다음엔 X.

### 부정 — say not / tell not

```
He said, "I am not tired."
→ He said (that) he was not tired.
```

부정 그대로 변환.

### 종합 예시

```
직접: She said to me, "I will visit you here tomorrow."
간접: She told me (that) she would visit me there the next day.

변환:
- said to me → told me
- will → would
- you → me (말하는 사람 시점)
- here → there
- tomorrow → the next day
```

## 대표 문장 (외우기)

> **He said to me, "I will come tomorrow." → He told me that he would come the next day.**

`said to me → told me` + `will → would` + `tomorrow → the next day`. 화법 변환 4 단계가 한 줄에.

## 실전 문제 5개

### 문제 1.
다음 직접화법을 간접화법으로 가장 자연스럽게 바꾼 것은?

`He said, "I am hungry."`

① He said that I am hungry.
② He said that I was hungry.
③ He said that he was hungry.
④ He said that he is hungry.

### 문제 2.
어법상 옳은 것은?

① He said me that he was tired.
② He told that he was tired.
③ He told me that he was tired.
④ He said me he was tired.

### 문제 3.
다음 직접화법을 간접화법으로 바꿀 때 가장 자연스러운 것은?

`She said, "I will go there tomorrow."`

① She said that she would go there tomorrow.
② She said that she would go there the next day.
③ She said that she will go there the next day.
④ She said that she goes there tomorrow.

### 문제 4.
다음 빈칸에 알맞은 것은?

`He said, "I met her _____ ."`
(직접 → 간접: He said (that) he had met her _____ .)

직접 yesterday → 간접 ?

① yesterday  ② the day before  ③ ago  ④ then

### 문제 5.
다음 우리말을 영어로 옮길 때 가장 적절한 것은?

> 그녀는 자기가 어제 그를 만났다고 말했다.

① She said that she meets him yesterday.
② She said that she met him yesterday.
③ She said that she had met him the day before.
④ She told that she had met him yesterday.

---

## 정답 + 해설

### 1. ③ He said that he was hungry.

I → he, am → was (backshift).

### 2. ③ He told me that he was tired.

told + 사람 목적어 me. say + me X.

### 3. ② She said that she would go there the next day.

will → would, tomorrow → the next day.

### 4. ② the day before

yesterday → the day before (또는 the previous day).

### 5. ③ She said that she had met him the day before.

said (과거) + met (과거) → had met (backshift). yesterday → the day before. ④ told 는 사람 목적어 필요.
